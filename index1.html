ABOUT OUR SUCCESS

KHUSHBOO KHANNA
FOUNDER CEO

NetCoreInfo Business Group is a vibrant organization soaring at the heights of success with both of its wings flocking in an array of Software Development as well as Education. Our Company, NITD is based out of New Delhi and Noida with our upcoming branches soon to be opened at Lucknow & Kolkata. NITD was founded by a bunch of enthusiastic IT & ITES freaks who wanted to overcome the stereotypic routine of the industry and establish a platform that would not only assist the market for business but also for the sake of Human resource in this technological world itself

One our one domain we employ highly qualified software development engineers, to take up the challenges in this wildly competitive world. While on the other hand, we train and nurture the newbie of this so called IT and Management world.

The Fresher- We Hire Them! We Train Them! We Deploy Them! We Make Their Career!

Therefore, the mission of the company was defined – To contribute towards forward Perception. The small journey of ours i.e. past 10 years has passed by in a blink of an eye for us. But yes, when I sit and recollect the memories of NITD, what brings smile on my face is few Milestones reached, some Targets achieved, many Client's Blushing faces, and a Lots of Promises Fulfilled. That's I think all I reconnect of mine from NITD.

When we count project domain to cater for our client, well here we have developed a range of solutions and gained ample experience: Custom Software Development, E-commerce Solutions Development, Learning Solutions Development, Web development, Legacy Applications Reconstruction, Project Recovery, Consulting, software language programs, and Quality Assurance, and I am sure a lot are missing from this list, Never Mind! NITD is known for its multi-faceted working domain expertise such as IT Consulting, Outsourcing, Industrial Training both in Corporate & In-House Institutional, Corporate Training, Vocational Training for Services Sectors and Education and Training in Schools.
